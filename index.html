Benefits of Orange Juice

5 Surprising Health Benefits of Orange Juice

Orange juice is enjoyed around the world.It’s made by squeezing oranges to extract the juice, either by hand or using commercial methods.It’s naturally high in vital nutrients, such as vitamin C and potassium. Plus, commercial varieties are often enriched with calcium and vitamin D. Nonetheless, there’s controversy regarding whether or not it contributes to a healthy diet. Here are 5 health benefits of orange juice. 1. Rich in Several Important Nutrients 2. High in Antioxidants 3. May Help Prevent Kidney Stones 4. May Improve Heart Health 5. May Decrease Inflammation
